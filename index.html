About Me

Przemyslaw
Krys

📍 SATS Sandvika Panorama · Oslo

I'm Krys — a personal trainer based in Oslo with a background that stretches from the gym floors of the UK to competitive strength sports and martial arts. Training has never just been a job for me. It's been a constant in my life, and that shows in how I work with people.

My coaching philosophy is simple: no shortcuts, no guesswork, no programmes pulled off the internet. Everything I write is built around the individual in front of me — their goals, their schedule, their history, and how their body actually responds to training. I use a structured, evidence-based approach that prioritises technique, smart progression, and long-term results over quick fixes.

Over the years I've coached people across a wide range of goals — fat loss, muscle building, strength development, HYROX race preparation, ultra-running conditioning, and general fitness. What ties all of it together is the same principle: train with intention, recover properly, and trust the process.

I work with clients in person at SATS Sandvika Panorama and online worldwide through the Everfit app. I coach in English and Polish, and take on a limited number of clients at a time to make sure everyone gets the attention they deserve.

What I Specialise In

Areas of Expertise

Strength Training

Not just lifting heavier — moving better. I build strength that transfers to real life, with technique-first programming that creates lasting results across barbell, kettlebell, and bodyweight work.

HYROX Coaching

Race day is earned in training. I specialise in HYROX-specific preparation — building the engine, developing strength endurance, dialling in pacing, and making sure you cross the finish line strong.

Coaching & Structure

Most people don't fail because they lack motivation — they fail because they lack a plan. I provide the structure, accountability, and direction that turns effort into measurable progress.

Kettlebell Training

One tool, unlimited potential. Kettlebell training is one of the most efficient ways to develop strength, endurance, and coordination simultaneously — and I've been coaching it for years.

Conditioning

A bigger engine changes everything. I use evidence-based conditioning methods to raise your aerobic ceiling and improve recovery — without grinding away the strength you've worked hard to build.

Movement & Body Awareness

Good movement is the foundation of everything. Drawing from years of martial arts practice, I help clients develop body control, spatial awareness, and the kind of movement quality that prevents injury and unlocks performance.

Online Coaching

How It Works

All online coaching runs through the Everfit app — available on iOS and Android.

01

We talk goals

You fill in a short intake form and we have a quick call to understand where you are, where you want to go, and what your schedule looks like.

02

Your plan lands in the app

I build your fully custom training program and deliver it via Everfit. Every exercise comes with a video demo so you always know exactly what to do and how to do it.

03

Track, log & message

You log workouts, track habits, and monitor nutrition directly in the app. I can see your progress in real time and you can message me any time.

04

Weekly check-in & adjust

Every week we review your data — performance, recovery, nutrition, habits. I adjust the program based on what's actually happening, not guesswork.
